### Additional resources:

Performance estimation problems were introduced in 2014 by **Yoel Drori** and **Marc Teboulle**, see [1]. This mini-class mostly follows the perspective and formalism and developments from [2, 3].  A friendly informal introduction to this formalism is available in this [blog post](https://francisbach.com/computer-aided-analyses/).

A longer version (>9h) of this course was taught at [SMAI-MODE](https://github.com/PerformanceEstimation/Tutorial-SMAI-MODE) by [Aymeric Dieuleveut](http://www.cmap.polytechnique.fr/~aymeric.dieuleveut/) and [Adrien Taylor](https://adrientaylor.github.io/). Performance estimation resources can be found at [here](https://github.com/PerformanceEstimation/). Do not hesitate to feedback and contribute if you want to share your thoughts or add your favorite examples!
